  2. High in fiber and water, slow to digest

    Fruits are rich in dietary fiber and water. They require time to chew and digest, which slows down sugar absorption and helps the body manage it safely.

  3. Excellent source of vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants

    Fruits provide essential nutrients like vitamin C, potassium, folic acid, and antioxidants. They also help reduce cholesterol and slow down carbohydrate absorption.

  4. May reduce the risk of chronic diseases

    Regular fruit intake is linked to lower risks of heart disease and diabetes. This could also be due to the healthier lifestyle often associated with fruit eaters.

  5. Helps with satiety and weight loss

    Fruits like apples and oranges are very filling -even more than eggs- so they help reduce overall food intake and can contribute to long-term weight loss.
